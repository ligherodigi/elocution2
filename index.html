ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Kemi Odukoya is an elocution coach, who is passionate about helping people to use their voices with power and precision. A graduate of The University of Manchester, she is the founder of The Elocution Studio.She believes that in an increasingly globalised world, where more and more people work and study cross-culturally, the ability to speak well, and be understood by anyone, is a vital skill.
